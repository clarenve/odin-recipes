Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made with layers of wide pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy ricotta or béchamel, and melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, all baked to perfection.

Ingredients needed

For the meat sauce:

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 pound of italian sausage, casing removed
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tabespoons sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

For the cheese mixture:

  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Other ingredients:

  • 1 package (16 ounces) lasanga noodles
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce:
    1. In a large pot over medium heat, cook 1 pound ground beef, 1 pound Italian sausage, and 1 large diced onion until the meat is browned and the onion is tender. Drain any excess fat.
    2. Add 4 minced garlic cloves and cook for another minute until fragrant.
    3. Stir in 2 cans (28 ounces each) crushed tomatoes, 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste, 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce, and 1/2 cup water. Mix well.
    4. Add 2 tablespoons sugar, 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil, 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon salt, and 1/2 teaspoon black pepper. Stir to combine.
    5. Bring the sauce to a boil, then reduce the heat and let it simmer for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the cheese mixture:
    1. In a medium bowl, combine 15 ounces ricotta cheese, 1 egg, 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt. Mix until well combined.
  3. Cook the Lasagna noodles:
    1. Cook 1 package (16 ounces) lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    3. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    4. Spread one-third of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
    5. Sprinkle one-third of the shredded mozzarella cheese (4 cups total) over the ricotta mixture.
    6. Add another layer of the meat sauce over the mozzarella cheese.
    7. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
    8. Sprinkle 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese evenly over the top layer of sauce.
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    1. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil (to prevent sticking, you can spray the foil with cooking spray or ensure it doesn't touch the cheese).
    2. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
    3. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the lasagna is bubbly and the top is golden brown.
    4. Let the lasagna cool for about 15 minutes before serving to allow it to set.

Enjoy your delicious, homemage Lasagna!
